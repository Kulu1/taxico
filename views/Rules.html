Rules and Regulations

1. User Conduct

Users are expected to maintain respectful conduct with drivers and other users.

Users must adhere to local traffic laws, follow safety guidelines, and avoid any behavior that compromises safety.

2. Service Usage

Users are accountable for the information provided in their accounts and must not share their login credentials.

Services must be used for legal and legitimate transportation purposes only.

3. Booking and Cancelations

All bookings are subject to availability and confirmation.

Users may cancel bookings according to the specified cancellation policy.

4. Payments

Users are responsible for the payment of booked services.

Additional charges may apply for extra services or changes in the itinerary.

5. Safety and Responsibility

Users are encouraged to report any issues or concerns promptly for resolution.

Users must not cause deliberate damage to the vehicle and must keep it clean during the ride.

6. Compliance with Terms

Users agree to abide by the Terms and Conditions and Rules and Regulations outlined by the service provider.

7. Accountability

Failure to comply with these rules and regulations may result in suspension or termination of service access.
