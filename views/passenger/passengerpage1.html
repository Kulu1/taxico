About Us

Aim

To develop a website that provides a more convenient and efficient way for customers to book and manage a cab.

Objectives

To help people easily get access to taxis during regular transit or emergencies.

Goals

To give our customers an easy and convenient booking experience.

Scope

Our scope is limited to the GCIT family.

TaxiCo is a system that allows both the driver as well as the passenger in making a better communication among each other where one can reach to their desired destination and the other can make an earning from it.

Our Services

Easy and Convenient Booking

Provide a simple and convenient booking process for our customers.

Safety and Security

Features like background checks for drivers.

Client Service

To deliver efficient and helpful customer service to both drivers and passengers.

Flexible Payment

The adaptability of the payment method, which allows users to pay with cash or a banking app after the ride.
